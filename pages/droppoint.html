Exact

Exact option generates you a completely random (x, y) drop point location.

City

City option chooses a random drop point from all available cities / named locations.

Extra

Extra option chooses a random point from unnamed locations of interest.

City + Extra

City + Extra -option is a combination of City and Extra -options.
